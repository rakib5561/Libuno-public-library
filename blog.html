Work plan - 2022

Library Professional Development Work

  • Library webpage will be updated as an effective, easy, and understandable access to DIU library resources and services to the users
  • Training programs on Dspace, Koha and MARC21 for building digital libraries will be held every semester during semester breaks
  • Starting a new program on the MA in Information Science and Library Management.
  • Training on open source software VuFind library search engine for DIU library personnel
  • Organizing a workshop on the Research Process and Publications for library professionals and final year students of DIU.
  • Publication of a newsletter in the DIU library.
  • CAS (Current Awareness Service) and SDI (Selective Dissemination of Information) for users
  • Information Literacy Program (Advance searching, Research Process and Library Orientation and Citation Style)
